Bacon Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Kimchi
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Green onion
  • Bacon

Steps üßë‚Äçüç≥

  1. Chop kimchi, bacon, and green onions. Loosen cooked rice.
  2. Cook bacon until crispy. Leave a bit of fat in the pan.
  3. Add kimchi and stir-fry for 3 minutes. Add sugar and red pepper powder.
  4. Add rice and stir well. Season with soy sauce and black pepper.
  5. Mix in green onions. Stir-fry briefly.
  6. Fry eggs in a separate pan.
  7. Serve rice topped with fried eggs.

Tips

Use well fermented kimchi for deeper flavor.

Add a bit of kimchi juice for extra richness.

Drizzle with sesame oil and sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds before serving.
